02 — About

Research Areas

Our lab explores core questions in attention and cognitive control through multidisciplinary approaches, creating new insights at the intersection of psychology, neuroscience, and computer science.

🧠

Computational Modeling

We computationally model human learning, reasoning, and decision-making processes using Bayesian inference and reinforcement learning frameworks.

👁️

Visual Perception & Attention

We study visual attention, scene recognition, and object perception mechanisms through eye-tracking and behavioral experiments.

🔬

fNIRS Neuroimaging

Using functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS), we measure and analyze real-time fronto-parietal network activation patterns during attentional and cognitive control processes.

🤖

Human-AI Interaction

We design intuitive AI systems based on human cognitive principles and study cognitive changes in humans when using AI tools.
